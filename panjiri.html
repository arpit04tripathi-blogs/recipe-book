Panjiri

Serves: 4
Prep time: 0 hours 10 minutes
Cook time: 1 hours 00 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • Aata
  • Dry Fruits (kaju, pista, badaam)
  • Elaichi
  • Sugar (powdered)

Method

  1. Prep Ingredients
  2. Cooking
recipe cooking

Published: Mar 2, 2021 by Parul Singh

Photo by Janko Ferlic from StockSnap

Prep Ingredients

  • Chopped dry fruits

Cooking

  1. Add Ghee in pan
  2. Add aata.
  3. Roast till brownish
  4. add elaichi, dry fruits, powdered sugar
  5. roast for some more time.
  6. Let it cool
